
📘 Topic: Gravity

📖 Definition:
Force that attracts objects toward each other

🔑 Key Points:
- Discovered by Isaac Newton
- Keeps planets in orbit
- Depends on mass and distance

🧠 Summary:
Gravity is a fundamental force governing motion in the universe.

- Who discovered gravity?
- What does gravity depend on?
